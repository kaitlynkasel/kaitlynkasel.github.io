Find out how to register to vote in upcoming elections, and other ways to get politically involved!

Upcoming Votes:

Two local votes upcoming in 2021 are the November general election and December recall election.

If you are registered to vote, you will receive a mail in ballot for voting.

If you aren't registered, you can register until Oct 25th. If you are at least 18 years old and a legal citizen and resident of Washington State, you can vote!
