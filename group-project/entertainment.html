An escape room, also known as an escape game, puzzle room or exit game, is a game in which a team of players discover clues, solve puzzles, and accomplish tasks in one or more rooms in order to accomplish a specific goal in a limited amount of time. The goal is often to escape from the site of the game. Multiple escape rooms are available at the Puzzle Masters Escapes, including Area 51, Book of Secrets, and What Happened in Vegas. Rooms are for 2-8 players and you have 60 minutes to solve the clues to escape.
