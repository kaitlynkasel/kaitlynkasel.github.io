Basic Information

Located in Washington, king county is the most populous county in the state, with over 2 million people living here. This county has a vibrant and active community with constant Events and activities to engage in. One of the first to be afflicted by the pandemic King County took extensive measures to ensure the safety of all of its residents and is now becoming familiar with regular day to day activities again. Public transportation is accessible and frequent, it is always easy to find something to do in King County.
