We exist to create exceptional theatrical productions that awaken the very best in children and teens. Broadway Bound Children’s Theatre transforms the lives of children ages 5 – 18 through active participation in a dynamic theatre arts program that combines a fun and supportive atmosphere with professional standards. Children of all skill levels and backgrounds are challenged to develop their mental, physical, emotional, social, and creative potential through the joy and discipline of live theatre.
