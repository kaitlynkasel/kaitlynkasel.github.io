Urge to Donate

Broadway Bound is a non-profit children’s theatre in Seattle which has given 23,000 kids the opportunity to participate in musical theatre after school. Year round, Broadway Bound has kids rehearsing 2-5 times a week to perform a vide variety of shows. To continue this practice we kindly ask for your donation. Broadway Bound Children’s Theatre transforms the lives of children ages 5 – 18 through active participation in a dynamic theatre arts program that combines a fun and supportive atmosphere with professional standards. Children of all skill levels and backgrounds are challenged to develop their mental, physical, emotional, social, and creative potential through the joy and discipline of live theatre; anything you are able to give is much appreciated.
